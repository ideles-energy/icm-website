Who we are

Independent Capacity Market is a collective of industry experts, BESS developers and flex traders who are missing a commercial layer. While the physical build-out of batteries is moving fast, capacity sits underutilised, pricing lacks transparency, and bilateral structures remain opaque. We believe in a new standard for market access in Europe — one that works for asset owners, aggregators, traders and grid operators alike.

What we do

The ICM auctions and executes the dispatch rights of flex assets, starting with Battery Energy Storage Systems in the Netherlands. Sellers list available capacity of qualified assets; the winning bidder gains real-time dispatch control of the asset for the contracted period. Our first auction is planned for Q3 2026 and will serve as a blueprint for what's to come.
