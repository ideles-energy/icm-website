Who we are

The Independent Capacity Market (ICM) is a collective of BESS developers, flex traders, grid operators and industry experts who are missing a commercial layer. While the physical build-out of batteries is moving fast, capacity sits underutilised, pricing lacks transparency, and bilateral structures remain opaque. We believe in a new standard for market access in Europe — one that delivers fair returns to asset owners, access to capacity for traders, and reliable flexibility for grid operators.

What we do

The ICM auctions the dispatch rights of flex assets and executes dispatch instructions, starting with BESS in the Netherlands. Sellers list available capacity of qualified assets; the winning bidder gains real-time dispatch control for the contracted period. The ICM is an independent facilitator with no commercial interest in auction outcomes, has an API-first set-up to minimize operational costs and continuously improves thanks to its AI-native architecture. Our first auction is planned for Q3 2026 and will serve as a blueprint for what's to come.
